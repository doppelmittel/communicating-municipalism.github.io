THIS IS MUNICIPALISM About Municipalism Municipalism stands for the idea of a political approach to community, which involves citizens in governing through participation. Either in collaborating with the state or create some kind of counter perspective. These initiatives confront social crises with courage and concrete utopias. They are interested in a profound and radical democratic change of society, working on a practice of solidarity for everyday live. Their agendas are broad drawing together resistance and creation across multiple times and places – from the smallest local context to the global scale. While acting on a local level of politics and participation is crucial and tries to reflect, that people, who are affected should hold the power. About the project We are an interdisciplinary team of students (University of Applied Sciences/University of Potsdam and Humboldt University Berlin) from diverse backgrounds and found each other during our course Mapping Cities, Making Cities which focusses on applied research and design at the intersection of data visualization and urban transformation.
